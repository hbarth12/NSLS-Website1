Director

Haddon Barth

Haddon Barth serves as Director of NSLS. A researcher focused on Syria and the Middle East, his work has appeared in Foreign Policy Magazine, the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace, Chatham House, the Middle East Institute, Lawfare, Al-Monitor, and other publications.

Before founding NSLS, Barth supported research at the International Crisis Group, the International Organization for Migration, and International IDEA. He graduated magna cum laude and Phi Beta Kappa from Princeton University with a degree in history.

He lives in Damascus and can be reached at hbarth@nsls.ngo.

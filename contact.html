Work With the Network

The Network for Syrian Legislative Studies is available to support Members of Parliament, committees, researchers, experts, universities, and institutional partners.

For Members of Parliament

Request Research or Expert Support

Members of Parliament and parliamentary committees may contact the Network to request research, legislative analysis, expert consultation, budget analysis, or policy briefings.

  • Explain a proposed law in plain language
  • Compare how another country addressed a similar policy issue
  • Assess likely implementation challenges
  • Review the fiscal implications of a reform
  • Identify relevant experts
  • Prepare a short committee briefing
  • Conduct background research on an emerging issue

The Network is independent and nonpartisan. We are committed to serving all Members of Parliament equally.

For Experts and Practitioners

Join the Expert Network

The Network welcomes expressions of interest from academics, former officials, policy practitioners, lawyers, economists, researchers, and technical specialists interested in contributing to evidence-based legislative support in Syria.

For University Partners and Students

Build a University Chapter

The Network works with university chapters that contribute policy research under structured editorial supervision. We welcome interest from students, faculty members, and university organizations interested in supporting rigorous, nonpartisan legislative research.

Impartiality Statement

Our Commitment to Impartiality

The Network for Syrian Legislative Studies is independent, nonpartisan, and committed to supporting all Members of Parliament equally.

We do not represent political parties, factions, candidates, or private interests. Our work is guided by evidence, comparative experience, transparent methodology, and a commitment to improving the quality of legislative decision-making.

Our goal is not to tell Parliament what political choices to make. Our goal is to ensure that Members of Parliament have access to the information and expertise needed to make those choices well.

For Members of Parliament

Contact the Network

Members of Parliament and parliamentary committees are welcome to contact NSLS with questions, research needs, or policy issues they would like to discuss.

The Network can help connect members with relevant research, comparative experience, and subject-matter expertise. Conversations can begin informally, and no detailed proposal is required.

For Experts and Practitioners

Join the Research Network

NSLS welcomes expressions of interest from academics, former officials, policy practitioners, lawyers, economists, researchers, and technical specialists interested in contributing to legislative research on Syria.

Members of the Research Network may review draft research, provide specialist advice, participate in briefings, or contribute expertise on specific policy questions.

Join the Research Network →

For Universities and Students

Work With NSLS

NSLS works with students, faculty members, research centers, and university organizations interested in supporting rigorous legislative research on Syria.

Universities may collaborate with the Network through student research chapters, supervised research projects, faculty engagement, events, or other forms of academic partnership.

Discuss a University Partnership →

For Student Leaders

Build a University Chapter

Students and faculty advisers interested in building a university chapter may contact NSLS to discuss research priorities, chapter structure, faculty support, and ways to contribute to legislative research projects.

University chapters help organize student research teams, connect projects with expert review, and expand the Network's capacity to support practical legislative research on Syria.
